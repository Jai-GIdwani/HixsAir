Who We Are

We are a group of passionate students from the HIXS Organization, committed to improving our environment and tackling the growing issue of pollution in Delhi NCR. As residents of this region, we’ve witnessed firsthand the detrimental effects of pollution caused by car emissions and factory discharges. Despite the government’s minimal actions to address these issues, we believe in the power of collective effort to drive change.

Our motto is simple: “To make our environment better.” We are determined to reduce pollution and raise awareness about the importance of environmental conservation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to take action against pollution by addressing its major cause:

  • Pollution from Cars – A leading cause of air pollution in Delhi NCR.

We are taking matters into our own hands through innovation, education, and activism. We aim to reduce pollution by creating sustainable solutions that not only help the environment but also empower local communities.

Why We Do It

Delhi NCR faces some of the worst air quality in the world, primarily due to unchecked industrial activities and an increasing number of vehicles on the road. We are committed to tackling this issue head-on by developing practical, eco-friendly solutions that can be implemented immediately and make a long-term impact.
